Welcome!

This site will guide you through our project as we seek to analysize religion through contextual literary devices in the lyrics of Hozier's debut album, "Hozier". Our goal is to gain a better understanding of Hozier and and how his work reflects his relationship with religion.

This project was done by Sarah Jacobson and Lauren Stanick, students in the Computational Methods and the Humanities course offered at the University of Pittsburgh.
