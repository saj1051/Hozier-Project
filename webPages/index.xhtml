Welcome!

This project was completed as part of the Computational Methods and the Humanities course offered at the University of Pittsburgh.

Lauren Stanick is a fifth year senior who will be graduating this spring with a Bachelor of Arts in Media and Professional Communications with a double minor in Theatre Arts and Creative Writing. She has some prior experience with HTML and CSS and was able to utilize those skills for structuring and styling the website. Lauren was in charge of website design, XSLT, and SVG coding for the project website.

Sarah Jacobson is a sophomore at the University of Pittsburgh studying English and political science. Prior to this course they had very minimal coding exposure. Sarah was in charge of SSI, Relax NG, textual analysis, placement of text on each page, and helped with website structure and styling.
